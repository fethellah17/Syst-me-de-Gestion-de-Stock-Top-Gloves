- 🟢 Green status badge (Case A)

- 🟢 Green text (accepted quantities)
- 🔴 Red text (defective quantities)
- Gray borders and backgrounds
- Colored boxes and fills

### AFTER (Black & White Only)
- ✅ No colored badges
- ✅ No colored text
- ✅ No colored borders
- ✅ No background fills
- ✅ Black text only
- ✅ Black 1pt separator lines
- ✅ Professional appearance
